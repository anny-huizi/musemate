About: Starry Night

What inspired this painting?

Vincent van Gogh painted Starry Night during his stay at the asylum of Saint-Paul-de-Mausole near Saint-Rémy-de-Provence. The night sky depicted in the painting suggests his continuing preoccupation with the imagination and dreams, despite his mental health struggles.

Museum Information

Visiting Information

Metropolitan Museum of Art

Hours: Sunday-Thursday: 10am-5pm, Friday-Saturday: 10am-9pm

Address: 1000 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10028

Admission: Adults: $25, Seniors: $17, Students: $12, Children under 12: Free

Website: www.metmuseum.org

Visitor Tips

  • Visit early in the morning to avoid crowds.
  • Coat check is available near the entrance.
  • Photography is allowed without flash in most galleries.
  • Free guided tours are available daily at 11am and 2pm.
  • The rooftop garden offers panoramic views of Central Park.
